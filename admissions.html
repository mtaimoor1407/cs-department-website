Admissions

Begin your journey in Computer Science. Find eligibility criteria, deadlines, and how to apply.

⏰ Fall 2024 Deadline: August 31, 2024. Apply early to secure your place.

Degree Programs

🎓 BSCS — Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

4-year undergraduate program. 130 credit hours. Covers software, AI, networks, and systems.

🎓 MSCS — Master of Science in Computer Science

2-year postgraduate program. Thesis or coursework track. Specializations in AI, Systems, or Security.

🎓 PhD in Computer Science

Research-intensive doctoral program. Minimum 3 years. Requires a strong research proposal.

How to Apply

1

Register Online

Visit admission.comsats.edu.pk and create your applicant account using your CNIC/passport number.

2

Fill the Application Form

Select BSCS/MSCS/PhD, choose Lahore Campus, and upload your educational documents.

3

Pay the Application Fee

Pay PKR 1,500 (BSCS) or PKR 2,000 (MSCS/PhD) via online banking or at any HBL branch.

4

Appear for Entry Test

Sit for the COMSATS Entry Test (Math, IQ, English). MSCS/PhD candidates also have an interview.

5

Check Merit List

Merit lists are displayed on the COMSATS website and notice boards within 2 weeks of the test.

6

Complete Enrollment

Pay semester fee, submit original documents, and collect your student ID to complete enrollment.
